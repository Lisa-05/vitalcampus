# 🌿 Vital Campus

Unified Platform for Student Wellbeing & Academic Support

---

## 📖 About

Vital Campus is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to support student wellbeing and academic success. It integrates mood tracking, crisis alert systems, counselling appointment booking, anonymous peer support forums, workload forecasting, and administrative analytics into one unified system.

**Problem Addressed:** Students face fragmented wellness resources, long counselling wait times, lack of mood tracking tools, and limited peer support options. Vital Campus solves this by providing a single, accessible platform.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### For Students
- ✅ **User Registration & Login** — Secure authentication with University SSO simulation
- ✅ **Mood Tracking** — Log daily mood on a 1-10 scale with sleep quality and stress triggers
- ✅ **Crisis Alert System** — Automatic alert when 3 consecutive low moods are logged
- ✅ **Appointment Booking** — Book counselling sessions with available counsellors
- ✅ **Anonymous Peer Support Forum** — Post and reply anonymously with category filtering
- ✅ **Workload Forecaster** — Predict high-stress weeks with personalised recommendations
- ✅ **Wellness Score** — Visual wellness indicator with progress tracking

### For Administrators
- ✅ **Admin Analytics Dashboard** — View system-wide usage statistics
- ✅ **User Management** — Overview of total users, mood entries, appointments, and forum posts
- ✅ **Export Reports** — Generate PDF and CSV reports
